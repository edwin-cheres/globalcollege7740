At GTTC (Global Technical Training College), we believe technical education is the foundation for real-world success. s a registered technical institute based in Kenya, our mission is to equip students with hands-on skills that are in demand across industries.

GTTC is more than just a college. It’s a skills hub built for dreamers, doers, and future professionals. Founded with a vision to close the gap between education and employability, GTTC has grown into a trusted name in technical education. GTTC is known for quality training, modern facilities, and a student-centered approach.

Our Vision

To be the leading technical training institute that empowers youth with employable skills and a mindset for innovation.

Our Mission

At GTTC, our mission is to empower students with hands-on, market-relevant skills that prepare them for the real world.

Our Core Values

Our core values include: Empowerment Through Skill, Integrity & Accountability, inclusivity and diversity.
